Japanese

By the time I left Japan I had a good grasp of the basics in Japanese. I had a Japanese girlfriend who I communicated with in Japanese the majority of the time. I also practiced Karate, the classes of which were all held in Japanese. I was able to make simple conversations with my coworkers at the various schools where I worked. One challenging aspect of the language is the formality levels; I would be able to have full converstaions with my girlfriend but communicating with others was more difficult since I wanted to address people with the appropriate level of formality. I can read (but not necessarily understand) and write Hiragana and Katakana fluently. My comprehension of Kanji has also improved since I began studying Chinese.
